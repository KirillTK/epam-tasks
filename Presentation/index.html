What is Scrum ?
Scrum is an agile project management methodology or framework used for software development projects with the goal of delivering new software capability every 2-4 weeks.

Disadvantages Waterfall Methodology
  • Difficult to go back and change something
  • Late developing software during the life cycle.
  • High amounts of risk
  • Bad model for a long projects.
  • Not suitable for the projects where requirements are changing
Advantages of Scrum Methodology
  • Higher productivity
  • Better-quality products
  • Reduced time to market
  • Quick start
  • Better team dynamics
  • Low amount of risk
Components of Scrum development
  • Team roles
  • Events
  • Artifacts
